Mahbles Counter

Rules

  • Mahbles are created during Mahble Games
  • Mahble Games can only occur once per night
  • Mahble Games can only occur with 3+ players
  • n/2 Mahbles, rounded down, are created and won during Mahble Games (e.g. 1 during 3 player games, 2 during 4 player games)
  • Earned Mahbles may be bet in a Betting Mahble Game
